    Leading the Future of Industrial IoT

    As an experienced Industrial IoT (IIoT) company, Hictros seamlessly integrates IoT technology to help you:

    • Monitor Key Metrics in Real-Time: Track Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and Key Health Indicators (KHIs) of your machines directly from the shop floor with our advanced IoT solutions.
    • Minimize Downtime with Predictive Maintenance: Proactively reduce equipment failures and downtime with our predictive maintenance capabilities.
    • Access Insights Instantly: View critical metrics such as OEE, OLE, and more on a single dashboard, eliminating the need for manual calculations.
    • Enable Seamless Connectivity: Connect tools, machines, and sensors to your network, enabling real-time data collection on downtime, inventory, and job status.
    • Unlock Operational Excellence: Benefit from task automation, improved efficiency, and enhanced productivity through our leading IoT platform.

    Real-Time Production Monitoring Accelerator

    • Seamless Integration: Effortlessly integrates machine and process data into your manufacturing ecosystem, capturing real-time insights across all parameters.
    • Enhanced Visibility & Insights: Gain instant access to key production metrics like rejection rates, rework instances, and loss analysis, driving up to a 30% reduction in rework and rejections.
    • Performance Optimization with KPI Tracking: Track critical KPIs such as OEE, OLE, uptime, idle time, and quality metrics to identify improvement areas and enhance production efficiency.
    • Streamlined Operations & Continuous Improvement: Boost overall productivity and asset utilization by 15-20% through streamlined operations and ongoing improvement initiatives.

    Condition-Based & Predictive Maintenance Accelerator

    • Advanced Condition Monitoring: Enables early detection of potential issues, allowing proactive intervention to prevent breakdowns and unplanned outages.
    • Real-Time Visibility & Alerts: Provides 24/7 monitoring through a centralized dashboard, offering key asset health insights (MTTR, MTBF). Immediate alerts enable quick action to minimize downtime and enhance reliability.
    • Predictive Maintenance Analytics: Utilizes advanced analytics to optimize maintenance schedules, cut costs, and extend asset lifespan by up to 50%.
    • Optimized Asset Performance: Enhances operational efficiency, reduces downtime, and ensures continuous availability of critical assets, driving competitiveness in the Industry 4.0 era.

    Smart Energy Management Accelerator

    • Comprehensive Energy Tracking: Monitors energy distribution across multiple sources (solar, wind, HVAC, bio-gas, gas, battery, genset) to provide actionable insights and optimization opportunities.
    • Net-Zero Energy KPI Monitoring: Tracks and analyzes key metrics to optimize energy usage, driving progress toward net-zero energy goals.
    • Demand Forecasting & Carbon Emission Analysis: Utilizes advanced analytics to predict energy demand, enhance resource allocation, and manage carbon emissions efficiently.
    • Custom Reporting & Seamless Integration: Delivers tailored reports and integrates with MES, ERP, and other systems for enhanced visibility and data-driven decision-making.

    Track & Trace Accelerator

    • Advanced Product Identification: Assigns unique identifiers (barcode/RFID) for precise tracking of materials across the supply chain.
    • Real-Time Data Capture & Quality Control: Enables real-time scanning and quality checks, ensuring compliance with FIFO/LIFO methodologies and product standards.
    • Actionable Insights & Optimization: Analyzes tracking data to improve inventory management, product movement, and quality control for enhanced efficiency.
    • Enhanced Demand Forecasting & Planning: Leverages historical data and trends to predict demand accurately, optimizing production planning.
